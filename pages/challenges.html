Code Challenges

Every Monday we release a code challenge here. End of the week we publish one or more solutions.

The goal is to have us and our readers work on the solution during the week.

Just fork our challenges repo and work on your solution locally. See here for more details.

If you have questions or want to share your solution use the comment sections of the articles below.

Remember, we don't strive for the 'best' solution, it's not a competition. The objective is learn more and better Python. We believe solving code challenges is a very effective way to achieve this.
Have fun!
